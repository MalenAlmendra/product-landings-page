CHARACTERISTICS

BLACK SPECIAL EDITION

Music lovers appreciate its natural, vibrant sound as well as its excellent spacious reproduction. The semi-open headphones attenuate ambient noise but do not exclude it completely. They thus convey a harmonious sound experience. These headphones have been part of the beyerdynamic portfolio for 20 years and still enjoy exceptional popularity. The classic in the 250-Ohm version is now also available in a Black Special Edition.
